Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp

Home

Description

Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp is a dish featuring shrimp marinated in a mixture of lime juice, spices, and often garlic or chili, then grilled until slightly charred and flavorful. The lime adds a bright, tangy taste, while the spices provide heat and depth, creating a zesty and savory seafood dish that is quick to cook and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Cajun seasoning (such as Emeril's Essence)
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp (30-40 per pound)
Steps
  • Step 1 - Mix together Cajun seasoning, lime juice, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Add shrimp, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for 20 minutes.
  • Step 2 - Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  • Step 3 - Remove shrimp from marinade; shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
  • Step 4 - Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 2 minutes per side.
  • Step 5 - Serve amd enjoy!
